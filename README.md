# Farsighted Literary Society – Community Guidelines  
*Last updated: 16 August 2025*

Welcome to the Farsighted Literary Society (FLS), a writing archive founded and run by middle-school students.  
These guidelines keep the space safe, legal, and inspiring for authors and readers of every age, profession, and nationality. They are written in plain English; no law degree required.

---

## 🔴 Red Zone – Never Allowed  
We remove these without warning and may ban repeat offenders.

1. **Anything illegal under PRC law**  
   Includes but is not limited to: content that endangers national security, leaks state secrets, spreads violence, pornography, or hate speech.
2. **Harassment & Harm**  
   Personal attacks, doxxing, threats, fraud, plagiarism, impersonation.

---

## 🟡 Amber Zone – Edit First  
We will ask you to revise before publication.

1. **Disturbing or adult themes without context or warning tags.**  
   *Tip: add a short note such as “Contains mild violence.”*
2. **Click-bait titles**  
   Example of what to avoid: “You Won’t Believe What Happened Next!!!” when the story is about losing a pen.
3. **Pure ads or spam**  
   Repeated low-quality posts, commercial promotion, referral codes.

---

## 🟢 Green Zone – We’d Love to Read It  
- Original fiction, essays, poetry, translations, book reviews, scripts, comics—anything creative.  
- Languages: Chinese, English, Japanese, or any mix you like.  
- Length: 300–8,000 words is the sweet spot, but we won’t set a hard limit if the piece holds our attention.

## 🔴 红灯区 绝对禁止  
一经发现，立即移除，屡犯者封禁。

1. **违反中国现行法律的内容**

2. **标题党**  
   例：标题写“震惊！”，正文只是丢了根笔。  
3. **广告或灌水**  
   重复发、发邀请码、发商品链接。

Bienvenue ! Ce guide est rédigé dans un français clair et s’adresse à tous : élèves, enseignants, parents, travailleurs et amis du monde entier.

---

Tout contenu ci-dessous est supprimé sans préavis ; récidive = bannissement.

   Exemples : atteinte à la sécurité nationale, pornographie, violence, incitation à la haine.

2. **Titres putaclics**  
   Ex. : « INCROYABLE ! Vous n’allez pas… » alors qu’il s’agit d’un stylo perdu.  
3. **Publicité et spam**  
   Répétitions, liens d’affiliation, annonces commerciales.
